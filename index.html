About Geeky-teacher

Hi! I'm Simon, and I've been teaching Computer Science for over 14 years, working with an incredibly diverse range of students - from disadvantaged communities in the UK to students with SEND (particularly SEMH and ASC), A-Level students in China, and now international school students here on the Costa del Sol.

This experience has taught me that every student can succeed with the right approach. Whether you're struggling with basic concepts, have specific learning needs, or are aiming for top grades, I adapt my teaching style to match how you learn best.

I believe in practical, project-based learning that goes beyond memorizing syntax. My students build real projects - games, websites, apps - while mastering the programming concepts they need for their exams. Whether it's Python fundamentals for GCSE or tackling A-Level coursework across different exam boards (AQA, OCR, Edexcel, CAIE), we'll work together to make computing click.

Currently based on the Costa del Sol, I offer both online tutoring worldwide and face-to-face sessions locally.
